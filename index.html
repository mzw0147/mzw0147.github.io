Profile

As a graduate research assistant at Auburn University, my research focuses on engineering systems in agriculture based on advanced AI applications. Previously, as a lecturer in the Department of Food Engineering, I specialized in artificial intelligence in food engineering. My research involves AI-based cyber-physical systems for smart agriculture, with expertise in controlled environment agriculture, robotics, machine learning, and image processing.
